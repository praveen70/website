SUMMER CAMP 2019 CLOSING CEREMONY

Shanti Niketan Vidyapeeth celebrated its summer camp closing ceremony on 04/06/2019, Tuesday, after running ten days exciting summer camp for the students to make the best use of their summer vacation.The closing ceremony also included wonderful displays of talent by the summer camp kids.During summer camp students had learned a lot under the supervision of their respective trainers. On the last day of the summer camp students had performed... various dance and sang the songs as well.At the end appreciation certificates were given the students and all students and staff moved towards swimming pool area, where they enjoyed pool party and swooped in pool. The ceremony was followed by the speech of Our Principal Ms. Vibha Gupta in which she motivated the students to focus on their hobbies as well including studies and told them Dos and Donts during the summer vacations.Students had full of fun and merriment all the days. The students all with smiles and cheers throughout the camp.
